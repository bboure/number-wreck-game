Privacy Policy

Application: Number Wreck  ·  Last updated: June 30, 2026

This policy describes how Benoit Bouré ("we", "us") handles information in the Number Wreck mobile application (Android and iOS).

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information directly.

The app stores the following data locally on your device only — it is never transmitted to us or any server:

  • Your high score
  • Sound preference (on/off)
  • Haptic feedback preference (on/off)

The app contains advertising provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may automatically collect the following information in order to serve ads:

  • Device advertising identifier (Google Advertising ID on Android, IDFA on iOS)
  • IP address and approximate location (derived from IP)
  • Device type, model, and operating system version
  • App usage and interaction data related to ads

2. How We Use Information

We do not use or process any personal data ourselves. The locally stored data (score and preferences) is used solely to remember your settings between sessions. Ad-related data is processed by Google AdMob to select and deliver advertisements.

3. Advertising and Third-Party Services

Number Wreck uses Google AdMob to display interstitial advertisements. Google's use of advertising identifiers is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

On iOS, the app requests permission to track you via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework before any personalised advertising identifier is accessed. You can change this permission at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

In the European Economic Area (EEA) and the United Kingdom, the app presents a consent form before showing ads, as required by the GDPR and the IAB Transparency and Consent Framework. You can withdraw or modify your consent at any time by contacting us (see section 6).

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. Ad serving data is shared with Google AdMob solely for the purpose of displaying advertisements, as described above.

5. Children's Privacy

Number Wreck is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

6. Your Rights

If you are located in the EEA or UK, you have rights under the GDPR including the right to access, correct, or delete your data, and the right to object to or restrict processing. Since we do not collect personal data directly, most of these rights apply to Google AdMob's data practices. You can:

  • Opt out of personalised ads via your device settings (Android: Google Settings → Ads; iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking)
  • Reset or delete your advertising identifier in device settings
  • Contact us at the address below for any other requests

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact
